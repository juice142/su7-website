The Xiaomi SU7 is Xiaomi Auto's first flagship intelligent electric sedan, combining ultimate performance, smart technology, and elegant design.

Dual-motor AWD version accelerates from 0-100 km/h in just 2.78 seconds with CLTC range up to 800 km, delivering supercar-level driving experience.

Intelligent Technology
